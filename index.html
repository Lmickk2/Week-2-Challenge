About Me

Originally a pen and paper artist, I was given the great idea to digitalize my artwork, and get into the world of NFTs. I built a project from the ground up, including developing (with helpful starter code, of course), community building, and marketing my artwork. This eventually led to my NFT collection completely selling out. It was after all of this, that I realized that the development portion of the project was my favorite. Fast forward a year, I am now enrolled in the Full Stack Developer Bootcamp at the University of Miami with the intention of becoming a full time developer in the near future.
